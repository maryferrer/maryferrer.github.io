About Me

Mary Ferrer is a Front-end web developer with over 4+ years of experience in customer-facing roles. I am passionate about front-end technologies and aim to create beautiful web applications with accessibility and best practices in mind.

My goal is to use my knowledge in responsive web design and my business acumen from sales work to build applications that are not only beautiful and accessible but also solve client problems.

Not only am I proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and React, I also have years of experience in client-side communication, cross-departmental collaboration and technical problem solving skills from working at large Biotech service providers.

I became interested in Frontend development because I've always loved the arts (dance, photography, crafting) and making beautiful things. This is a way to explore my deep interest in design and art while also creating interfaces that people enjoy using.

At the root of it all, I am a student. I welcome challenges and will contininue to learn new technologies while improving my current skillset. If I don't know it, I will learn it!
