How to make a PB & J Sandwich

  1. Toast two pieces of bread.
  2. Slather peanut butter on one piece of toast.
  3. Slather jelly on the second piece of toast.
  4. Bring both pieces together, jellied sides facing together.
  5. Enjoy!
